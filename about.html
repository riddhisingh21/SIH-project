About SignScribe

At SignScribe, we are dedicated to bridging communication gaps for individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing. Our mission is to create an inclusive society where everyone, regardless of their hearing ability, can communicate seamlessly and effectively. Through innovative technology, we offer a tool that translates Indian Sign Language (ISL) gestures into real-time text and speech, allowing people to interact and engage with the world around them.

We believe that communication is a fundamental right, and we strive to empower individuals with hearing impairments by providing a platform that makes conversations more accessible, inclusive, and effortless.
